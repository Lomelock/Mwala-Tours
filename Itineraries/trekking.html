Trekking from Ngorongoro Highland to Lake Natron

Nature lovers this is special for you

One of our most popular treks is the Empakai Crater to Lake Natron trek. In three days and two nights, you will experience the misty forests and rolling hills, ancient craters and the noble Maasai who inhabit the area. Discover a land only dreamed of in fantasy, where a communion of contrasts awakening the senses and rejuvenating the spirit of adventure that we are all born with.

Tour Details

  • Duration: 4 Days / 3 Nights

  • Included: Safari, all meals, transportation, and guided tours
  • Excluded: Flights and travel insurance

Itinerary
