Ngorongoro Highland Safari

3-Day Ngorongoro Crater Safari Itinerary

Experience the Heart of Africa’s Wildlife Paradise

Tour Details

  • Duration: 5/4 Nights
  • Price: Starting from $3,500 per person
  • Accommodation: Luxury lodges and camps
  • Included: Full safari, all meals, game drives, and transfers
  • Excluded: Flights and travel insurance

Day 1: Arusha to Ngorongoro – Journey to the Crater Highlands

Depart Arusha in the morning with your private guide and 4x4 safari vehicle. Travel through the lush highlands of northern Tanzania, enjoying scenic landscapes as you ascend towards the Ngorongoro Conservation Area.

Arrive in time for lunch at Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge, perched dramatically on the crater rim with panoramic views into the caldera. After settling in, enjoy a guided nature walk or cultural visit to a nearby Maasai village.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge
Meals: Lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Full-Day Crater Safari – Into the Wildlife Bowl

After an early breakfast, descend 600 meters into the Ngorongoro Crater for a full-day game drive. Known as “Africa’s Garden of Eden,” the crater teems with wildlife including lions, elephants, buffaloes, rhinos, zebras, and flamingos by the soda lake.

Enjoy a picnic lunch near the hippo pool and continue exploring the diverse habitats of this World Heritage Site. In the late afternoon, ascend back to the rim for dinner and a sunset view from the lodge.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge (or upgrade to The Highlands Ngorongoro)
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 3: Return to Arusha – Farewell to the Wild

Wake up to misty crater views and enjoy a relaxed breakfast. On the journey back to Arusha, you may choose to stop at Lake Manyara viewpoint or Mto wa Mbu village for a quick cultural experience or shopping for local crafts.

Arrive in Arusha by late afternoon, marking the end of your unforgettable Ngorongoro safari.

Meals: Breakfast & Lunch
