Northern Circuit Safari

Explore the Breathtaking Northern Circuit

The Northern Circuit of Tanzania is one of the most sought-after safari destinations, offering an incredible range of wildlife and spectacular scenery. From the Great Wildebeest Migration in Serengeti to the UNESCO World Heritage Site Ngorongoro Crater, the Northern Circuit is a must-visit destination for any safari enthusiast.

Tour Details

  • Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights
  • Price: Starting from $2,500 per person
  • Accommodation: Luxury lodges and camps
  • Included: Full safari, all meals, game drives, and transfers
  • Excluded: Flights and travel insurance

Itinerary

Click each day below to view more details:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Arusha - Meet your guide, transfer to the hotel, and relax.
  • Day 2: Tarangire National Park - Full-day safari exploring the iconic baobab trees and elephant herds.
  • Day 3: Lake Manyara National Park - Morning safari to see tree-climbing lions and vibrant birdlife.
  • Day 4: Serengeti National Park - Game drive en route to the Serengeti, followed by an afternoon safari.
  • Day 5: Serengeti National Park - Full-day safari witnessing the Great Migration and diverse wildlife.
  • Day 6: Ngorongoro Crater - Morning safari in the crater, home to the Big Five and stunning landscapes.
  • Day 7: Return to Arusha - Morning relaxation and transfer to the airport for departure.
