Serengeti Safari

5-Day Serengeti Safari | Witness the Great Migration in Tanzania

Experience the thrill of a lifetime with our 5-day Serengeti Safari, where you will witness the breathtaking Great Migration. This tour takes you through the heart of Tanzania's most iconic national park, the Serengeti, known for its stunning landscapes and incredible wildlife. From the vast plains to the river crossings, this safari offers an unforgettable adventure for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts alike.

Tour Details

  • Duration: 5/4 Nights
  • Price: Starting from $3,500 per person
  • Accommodation: Luxury lodges and camps
  • Included: Full safari, all meals, game drives, and transfers
  • Excluded: Flights and travel insurance

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arusha to Serengeti (Central): Your adventure begins with an early morning flight or drive from Arusha to Seronera Airstrip in the Central Serengeti. Upon arrival, your guide will meet you and take you on a game drive en route to your lodge. The Central Serengeti is rich with wildlife year-round, offering early glimpses of lions, elephants, and herds of wildebeest. Accommodation: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge Meals: Lunch and Dinner.
  • Day 2: Full-Day Safari in Central Serengeti: Spend the day exploring the game-rich Seronera Valley, known for its abundance of predators like leopards and lions. Your guide will take you to prime viewing spots where the migrating herds gather. Enjoy a picnic lunch in the bush before continuing your game drive through the golden savannah. Accommodation: Kubu Kubu Tented Lodge Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner.
  • Day 3: Transfer to Northern Serengeti (Kogatende): Today, you head north toward the Mara River, the site of the dramatic river crossings during the Great Migration. Along the way, witness sprawling plains filled with migrating wildebeest and zebra. Arrive in time for an evening game drive in the northern region. Accommodation: Mara Under Canvas Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner.
  • Day 4: Game Drives in Northern Serengeti: Spend the day exploring the Mara River area, where the migration reaches its peak between July and October. With luck, you'll witness the incredible spectacle of thousands of wildebeest braving the crocodile-filled river. Look out for big cats, elephants, and birds of prey as you move across this wildlife corridor. Accommodation: Mara Under Canvas Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner .
  • Day 5: Serengeti to Arusha (Departure): Enjoy a final early morning game drive before catching your flight back to Arusha. You may also choose to extend your trip to visit Ngorongoro Crater or Zanzibar for some relaxation. Meals: Breakfast.
