Lake Natron Adventure

Discover the Stunning Beauty of Lake Natron

Lake Natron, located in the northern part of Tanzania, is a unique and photogenic destination. Known for its stunning red-colored waters and its rich birdlife, this lake offers one of the most unforgettable adventures in Tanzania.

Tour Details

  • Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
  • Price: Starting from1,800 per person
  • Accommodation: Comfortable campsites and lodges
  • Included: Safari, all meals, transportation, and guided tours
  • Excluded: Flights and travel insurance

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Arusha and transfer to Lake Natron.
  • Day 2: Explore Lake Natron and its flamingo breeding grounds.
  • Day 3: Visit Ol Doinyo Lengai volcano and surrounding landscapes.
  • Day 4: Morning hike and return to Arusha.
